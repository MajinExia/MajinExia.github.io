A brief blurb about myself

Hi! I'm Luke and I'm a UX and Graphic Designer who believe that design can be a subtle yet impactful way to craft meaningful connections between people and the world around them through blending narratives, functionalities and overall commitment to social good.

Recently graduated from The School of Interactive Arts and Technology, I have experience working at the SFU Summer Festival as their social media coordinator and graphic designer. I have also worked at Fraser Health as a Digital Media Communication Specialist where I designed various graphics to advocate for Indigenous Health across the lower mainland.

Outside of design, you can often find me pursuing photography (don't mind me just plugging my Instagram here), playing video games (My favorite game series currently are JRPGs such as Xenoblade Chronicles 3, The Yakuza Series and Honkai Star Rail), or watching Japanese Anime (my favorite in recent time was The Dangers in My Heart).
