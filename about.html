About me

Hi, I'm Luke and I'm a Motion and Graphic Designer from Vancouver, Canada. I enjoy exploring different ways of breathing in life into what would otherwise be just a static image.

Currently, I'm pursuing my undergraduate degree at SIAT (The School of Interactive Arts and Technology) at Simon Fraser University. While during my uni courses, I'm also volunteering at the SFU Summer Festival as their social media coordinator and graphic designer. I have also worked at Fraser Health as a Digital Media Communication Specialist where I designed various graphics to advocate for Indigenous Health across the lower mainland.

When I'm not busy designing or doing my course work, you can often find me taking landscape photography (don't mind me just plugging my Instagram here), playing video games (currently suffering from Apex Legends and Genshin Impact addiction), or watching Japanese Anime (my favorite from this season is Lycoris Recoil).

Thank you for checking out my works!
