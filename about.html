About me

Hi! I'm Luke and I'm a Product and UI/UX Designer who enjoys exploring different ways of breathing in life into what would otherwise be a static image to enhance people's day to day experience. I believe that design can be a subtle yet impactful way to craft meaningful connections between people and the world around them through blending narratives, functionalities and overall commitment to social good (as you can never have too much positivity !)

Recently graduated from The School of Interactive Arts and Technology, I have been volunteering at the SFU Summer Festival as their social media coordinator and graphic designer. I have also worked at Fraser Health as a Digital Media Communication Specialist where I designed various graphics to advocate for Indigenous Health across the lower mainland.

Outside of design, you can often find me pursuing photography (don't mind me just plugging my Instagram here), playing video games (My favorite game series currently are The Yakuza Series and Honkai Star Rail), or watching Japanese Anime (my favorite in recent time was The Dangers in My Heart).

Thank you for checking out my works!
